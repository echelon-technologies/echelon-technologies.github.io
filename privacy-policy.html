Privacy Policy

Ensuring data transparency and safety for schools, parents, and students.

How we handle Your Information

Effective Date: May 1, 2020

1. Data Sovereignty & No-Storage Policy

SchoolByte is a Cloud-based School ERP designed to simplify school management. We operate on a No-Data-Storage principle for our own use; we do not collect, sell, or profile student or teacher data. All information remains the exclusive property of the educational institution using our services.

2. WhatsApp & Communication Safety

To comply with Meta Business standards, we confirm that any communication via WhatsApp is used strictly for responding to service inquiries, providing demos, or sending school-authorized notifications. We do not use your contact information for unsolicited marketing or share it with third-party brokers.

3. Role-Based Data Access

Our system uses secure role-based access for Admins, Teachers, Parents, and Accountants[cite: 1]. This ensures that sensitive student marks or financial data are only visible to authorized personnel, preventing unauthorized data exposure[cite: 1, 2].

4. Financial Transaction Security

For fee payments, we integrate with secure payment gateways to ensure cashless transactions without human error[cite: 1]. SchoolByte does not store credit card or bank details on its servers.

5. Security Standards

As a Cloud-based application, we utilize high-level encryption to ensure that data moving through the platform is never intercepted or leaked, providing access from anywhere in the globe securely[cite: 1, 2].
